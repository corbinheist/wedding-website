How do I get there?

Ballstad is a remote village in the Lofoten Archipeligo of Norway.

we strongly recommend connections through Bodø, NOR which is serviced by direct connections to OSLO by both SAS and Widerøe Airlines. From BDO, there are serveral options: Connecting Flight to Leknes, about a 15 minute drive from Hattvika Lodge. Ferry Service from Bodo to Moskenes, and then about a 90 minute drive through some of the most epic scenery the world has to offer.
