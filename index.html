Isn't it Cold in Norway?!

Well, yes. However, Lofoten's proximity to the terminus of the Jet Stream yields warmer (and moodier) weather.

Guests should plan for an unpredictable, yet mildly temperate climate. Daytime temperatures do not usually drop below freezing, and its not expected that there will be snow on the ground during the time we are in Lofoten.

Don't worry though, we have planned for contingencies, and in the event of inclement weather we will move the proceedings inside.

Why Ballstad and Norway?

Both Noam and Corbin are #Blessed to have been extroardinarily well travelled. Between the two of them, they have passports covering 3 continents. Travel is a key element of their lives, and a keystone of their relationship. In planning their wedding, they realized that they would ba asking someone to travel halfway across the world. So why not go somewhere that sparks joy, curiosity, and that insatiable desire to go to the edges of the map.

Friends, and our connection to people drew us to Lofoten. First, Corbin drove from Oslo to Ballstad with his friend (and this wedding's Photographer) Joel. Exactly 24H after leaving Oslo, they landed on the shores of Moskenes.

Shortly after, in October 2024, Corbin and Noam returned (Joel wasn't far away, in Nusfjord (editors note: GO THERE!)) to Hattvika for Vacation and fell in love all over again. With the wild, rugged beauty of this land where the sun goes and comes back again, and where you'll meet the kindest norwegian Hosts.

How do I get there?

Ballstad is a remote village in the Lofoten Archipeligo of Norway.

We strongly recommend connections through Bodø, NOR which is serviced by direct connections to Oslo and Copenhagen by both SAS and Widerøe Airlines.

From Bodø, there are serveral options:

  • Corbin & Noam highly recommend the Wood Hotel as the perfect stopover during your time in Bodø.
  • Connecting Flight to Leknes, about a 15 minute drive from Hattvika Lodge. Possibility for Hattvika Shuttle Service (pre-booked)
  • Ferry Service from Bodo to Moskenes, and then about a 90 minute drive through some of the most epic scenery the world has to offer.
